AAO - An STF's Initiative

Driving revenues with data, analytics and automation in all aspects of the task force business.

From Dr. AP Singh’s announcement in 2018: “Designing and delivering industry leading analytics, automaton and intelligence to our internal teams remains the highest priority for this task force, one that will determine our competitive viability in the years to come. AAO will oversee our efforts to integrate Operations and the firm's vast data resources to provide new intelligence to support real-time decision-making and build productivity solutions to make the government the most efficient competitor in the country.”


Scope

AAO is the result of joint work between STF & Management, Technology and Operations – plus government wide teams.
